void neon::Tetrahedron4::precompute_shape_functions ( )

Initialize the shape functions to the following polynomials

\begin{align*} N_1(r, s, t) &= r \\ N_2(r, s, t) &= s \\ N_3(r, s, t) &= t \\ N_4(r, s, t) &= 1 - r - s - t \end{align*}
